Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat butter in a pan, add onions and saut√© until soft.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, then add tomato puree and cook until thick.
  3. Add spices and salt. Mix well.
  4. Add paneer cubes and stir gently.
  5. Pour in cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with naan or rice.
