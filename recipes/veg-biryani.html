Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp biryani masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak rice for 20 minutes. Cook till 90% done and set aside.
  2. Heat oil, add whole spices and sliced onions. Sauté until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, then tomatoes and cook till soft.
  4. Add chopped veggies, spices, and salt. Cook for 5–7 minutes.
  5. Layer rice and veggie mix in a pot. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with raita or salad.
